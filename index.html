To ensure fair play and fun for everyone, please read and follow the rules carefully:

1. Team Leader Rule

Only the team leader is allowed to use a mobile phone during the hunt. Other members must not use their phones at any point.

2. Respect Volunteers

Any conflict, argument, or disrespectful behavior towards the volunteers or organizers will lead to immediate disqualification.

3. Progression Rule

Teams that do not qualify for the next round will not be given a second chance under any circumstances.

4. Clue Confidentiality

If you come across a clue meant for another team, do not share, tamper with, or reveal it to anyone. Keep the spirit of the game alive!

5. Final Decision

In case of any dispute or confusion regarding results, the decision of the organizing club will be final and binding.

6. Team Conduct

  • No fighting, arguing, or blaming among team members.
  • Work together and maintain team spirit.
  • Any team found cheating or sabotaging others will be disqualified.

7. Respect the Venue

  • Do not damage property or litter.
  • Follow all instructions given by the organizers.

8. Time Management

Each round will have a time limit. Teams failing to complete within the given time will be eliminated automatically.

üß≠ How the Game Works

1

Starting Point

All teams will begin the treasure hunt together at the same location.

2

Team Pairing

  • Teams will be paired into groups ‚Äî two teams per group.
  • For example, Team A and Team B form one group, Team C and Team D form another, and so on.
3

Clues and Riddles

Each paired group will receive the same clue or riddle. The two teams in a group will compete against each other to solve it first.

4

Tech Question Unlock

  • Before every riddle, you will face a tech-related question.
  • You must answer this question correctly to unlock the riddle.
  • Once unlocked, you can begin searching for and solving the riddle.
5

Advancement

The team that solves the riddle first and enters the correct answer on the official website will move to the next round. The other team in that group will be eliminated from the hunt.

6

Round Progression Rule

  • The next round will only begin once all the winners from the previous round are finalized.
  • For example, if there are 20 teams in Round 1, the round will continue until 10 winning teams are confirmed.
  • If both teams in a pairing fail to finish within the given time limit, both will be disqualified.
7

Rounds Structure

The treasure hunt will have four stages in total:

  • Level 1 ‚Äì 25 minutes
  • Level 2 ‚Äì 25 minutes
  • Level 3 ‚Äì 25 minutes
  • Final Level ‚Äì 25 minutes
8

Winning Criteria

  • When you reach the location of the riddle, you will find a code, word, or phrase that serves as the answer required to move forward.
  • You must enter the exact answer on the official website ‚Äî every letter, space, and symbol must match perfectly.
  • Only if your answer is 100% correct will you be able to proceed to the next round.
  • The team that successfully clears all rounds and solves the final riddle first will be declared the winner of the Treasure Hunt!
